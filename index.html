About Our Company

SK UPVC Windows and Doors is a trusted UPVC installation company based in Thondamuthur, Coimbatore. We specialize in designing and installing high‑quality UPVC doors, windows, sliding systems, and mosquito mesh solutions for homes, apartments, and commercial spaces. Our mission is to provide customers with modern designs, long‑lasting durability, and affordable pricing.

With years of hands‑on experience in the industry and thousands of completed projects, our team focuses on precision measurement, strong materials, and clean professional installation. Every project is handled with attention to detail so that customers receive windows and doors that are stylish, secure, and built to last.

At SK UPVC Windows and Doors, we believe that good design improves comfort and value for every home. That is why we combine quality materials, expert workmanship, and reliable service to deliver results that our customers can trust.
